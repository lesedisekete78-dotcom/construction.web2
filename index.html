FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

How long does a typical construction project take?

Project timelines vary based on size and complexity. A typical residential renovation takes 4-8 weeks, while new construction can take 6-12 months. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Coglan Construction and Projects is fully licensed and insured. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation to protect both our team and our clients.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we offer free, no-obligation estimates for all projects. Our team will visit your site, discuss your requirements, and provide a detailed quote within 24-48 hours.

What are your working hours?

We are open Monday through Friday and close at 5pm. For emergency services or after-hours consultations, please contact us at 072 286 9783.

Do you handle permits and approvals?

Yes, our team handles all necessary permits and regulatory approvals. We work closely with local authorities to ensure your project complies with all building codes and regulations.
